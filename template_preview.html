UNSUNG INDIA

by Ikshana

The Forgotten Artisans of Kutch

Exploring the intricate handicrafts and the stories of artisans preserving centuries-old traditions in the remote villages of Kutch, Gujarat. These skilled craftspeople continue to create stunning textiles, pottery, and jewelry using techniques passed down through generations.

Living Bridges of Meghalaya

Discover the incredible living root bridges of Meghalaya, hand-shaped by the Khasi and Jaintia tribes over decades using the roots of rubber fig trees. These architectural marvels can withstand the region's heavy monsoons and grow stronger with time, some lasting for centuries.

The Last Manuscript Keepers

Meet the families in Varanasi who have been safeguarding ancient Sanskrit manuscripts for generations, preserving knowledge that dates back thousands of years. These dedicated custodians maintain fragile palm leaf and handmade paper manuscripts containing rare texts on philosophy, medicine, astronomy, and literature.
